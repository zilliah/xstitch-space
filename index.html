Cross Stitch Space

Cross stitch community, marketplace, project tracker, and database

Cross Stitch Space will be a free community space for cross stitchers and pattern designers, similar to Ravlery's model. It's currently in development - sign up below for updates!

For stitchers

  • Keep track of your projects, floss, and notions
  • Search, buy and wishlist patterns.
  • See what you can make using only the floss in your stash
  • Connect with other cross stitchers
  • Help build our pattern and floss database

For designers

  • Upload your patterns to sell on-site
  • Link patterns from your website/Etsy/etc to add to our database
  • We give you a space to sell your patterns without having to build your own website (or pay Etsy's high fees)
