X Stitch Space

Community space | Marketplace | Project Tracker | Pattern Database

X Stitch Space is a free community space for cross stitchers and pattern designers, similar to Ravelry's model. It's currently in development - sign up for updates:
Are you interested in beta testing?

For stitchers

  • Keep track of your projects, floss, and notions
  • Search, buy and wishlist patterns.
  • See what you can make using only the floss in your stash
  • Connect with other cross stitchers
  • Help build our pattern and floss database

For designers

  • A space to sell your patterns without building your own website (or paying Etsy's high fees)
  • Upload your patterns to sell on-site
  • Link patterns from your website/Etsy/etc to add to our database
  • Connect with other designers

Interested in helping make this? Join me on Github!
